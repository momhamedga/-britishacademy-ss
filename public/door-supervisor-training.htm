SIA Door Supervisor Training Courses We Offer

We provide several SIA accredited courses in Wembley, tailored to both new entrants and existing security professionals seeking top up training.

1. SIA Door Supervisor Course

The SIA Door Supervisor training is the main qualification required to obtain a Door Supervisor licence in the UK. This course covers:

  • Roles and responsibilities of a door supervisor 

  • Health and safety regulations

  • Conflict management and de-escalation techniques

Emergency procedures and basic first aid

Successful completion allows you to apply for your Door Supervisor licence through the SIA.

This SIA Door Supervisor top up training ensures you remain compliant with current regulations and maintain professional standards.

2. Door Supervisor Refresher Training

For those whose licence is approaching expiry, our Door Supervisor refresher training helps update skills without requiring the full course to be repeated.

Benefits include:

  • Quick updates on legal and procedural changes 

  • Reinforcement of conflict management skills

  • Preparation for licence renewal

3. Security Guard Refresher Training

We also offer Security Guard refresher training to help security personnel keep their skills sharp and up to date, ensuring continued compliance with SIA standards.

How Our Training Works

We understand that security roles require both theoretical knowledge and practical expertise. Our Door Supervisor course in the UK is designed to balance classroom learning with hands on exercises.

Course Structure
